Save Lives Stay Home

Please share these images wherever you think it will encourage people to practice social distancing and protect the most vulnerable people during the COVID-19 global health crisis.

To share an image with others, press and hold the image until you see a save or share option. On a laptop or desktop, right click and select save.

Now share the image with friends, family and coleagues on social media like Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, and LinkedIn or by messenger services like WhatsApp, iMessage or SMS text message.
